# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Bring a Trailer Land Cruiser Price Prediction service and related GPT Action API.

## What We Collect

When you use the service, we may collect and process:

- Listing URLs you submit (for example, Bring a Trailer listing links).
- Request metadata needed to operate the API (such as timestamps and basic technical logs).
- Prediction outputs generated by the model.

## How We Use Data

We use submitted data only to:

- Retrieve listing content required for feature extraction.
- Generate sale-price predictions and explanations.
- Monitor reliability, debug issues, and improve service quality.

## Data Sources

The service may retrieve publicly available listing data from Bring a Trailer pages provided by the user.

## Data Retention

- We do not intentionally store personal profiles about users.
- Operational logs may be retained for a limited period for security and debugging.
- We do not sell user data.

## Sharing

We do not share user-submitted data with third parties except:

- Infrastructure/service providers required to host and run the API (for example, Render).
- Legal compliance if required by law.

## Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect service data in transit and at rest, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## Children

This service is not directed to children under 13.

## Your Choices

Do not submit confidential or sensitive personal information in prompts or URLs.
